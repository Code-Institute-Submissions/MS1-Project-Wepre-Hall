Visit

The park contains a visitor centre, large children's playground, outdoor gym, football pitches, fishing ponds, woodland walking, nature trails, toilets and recreation area. The park is popular with dog walkers and includes free car parking.

The park has a number of landmarks including Ewloe Castle, The Red Rocks and the waterfall so please download the leaflet at the bottom of the page for more information.
